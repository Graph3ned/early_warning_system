# One-Time Post-Deployment Evaluation Questionnaire  
## IoT-Based Early Warning and Response System for Marine Fish Cage Aquaculture

**Purpose:** This is a **one-time survey questionnaire** conducted after the system has been deployed at the marine fish cage site. It is administered once to collect feedback from individuals directly involved in the daily operation and management of the cage. Your responses will be used to evaluate the system’s usability and effectiveness as an early warning and decision-support tool for fish kill prevention. There are no follow-up surveys; this single evaluation captures your experience with the deployed system.

**Respondent type:** (Please check one)  
- [ ] Fish cage owner  
- [ ] Aquaculture technician  

**Instructions:** This questionnaire is to be completed **once**, after you have used the system. Rate each statement based on your experience with the deployed system. Use the scale below unless otherwise indicated.

**Scale:**  
1 = Strongly disagree  
2 = Disagree  
3 = Neutral  
4 = Agree  
5 = Strongly agree
